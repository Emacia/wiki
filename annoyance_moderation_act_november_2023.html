Annoyance Moderation Act, November 2023

Any citizen may lodge a formal complaint of annoyance against a fellow citizen to the Judiciary. The plaintiff must provide the reason of his annoyance. The defendant will also have the opportunity to contest the claim if he so wishes. The Judiciary will vote on the defendants guilt. A conviction will count as a strike. If a citizen acquires three strikes then they shall be banned from the server. In the case the complaint is against a presiding Justice, then said Justice will be exempt from the voting procedure.
