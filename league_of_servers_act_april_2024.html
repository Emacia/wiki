League of Servers Act, April 2024

Article I: Purpose
  1. The purpose of this act is to establish diplomatic relations between Discordia and the League of Servers, a union comprising over 80 nations, to foster international cooperation, communication, and protection.
Article II: Appointment of Delegates
  1. The Minister of Foreign Affairs shall be responsible for appointing delegates to represent Discordia within the League of Servers.
  2. Delegates shall be chosen based on their diplomatic skills, knowledge of international affairs, and commitment to Discordia's interests.
Article II: Appointment of Delegates
  1. A dedicated channel shall be created within Discordia for League of Servers adverts to facilitate communication, coordination, and collaboration between Discordia and other member nations.
