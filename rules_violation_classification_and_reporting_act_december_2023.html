Rules Violation Classification and Reporting Act, December 2023

I/ Classification

1/ A felony is to be considered as the violation of any of the rules listed out in the Server Rule Reform Amendment Act or the Discordian constitution.

2/ A misdemeanour is to be considered as the violation of any other legislation passed by parliament.

II/ Reporting

1/ A felony is to be reported to the attorney general by a moderator and must always be prosecuted in court. Said moderator would have to provide evidence to the attorney general to be used for the conviction of the accused. If a moderator is not aware of a rule being broken, any citizen point out the violation to a moderator.

2/ A misdemeanour can only be reported to the attorney general by a moderator who must provide evidence of said violation. The attorney general has the right to choose wether or not the case is to be presented to court. Only a victim of a misdemeanour can point out the violation to a moderator if they have not already been made aware.
