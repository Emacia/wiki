Presidium Resignation Act, April 2024

Purpose: Due to not having a formal law about the resignation of presidium members, this act was written to solve this issue.

Legislation: For a Presidium to resign, they shall stand before parliament, where they have to give a formal reason of their resignation, upon accepting their resignation, parliament shall promptly remove said presidium from the office, and a by election shall happen on a time that parliament choose.
