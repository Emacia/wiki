Introduction

The Chancellor is the Head of State of Discordia and occupies the highest elected position within the server. The Chancellor is directly elected by the people at general elections.

History

The idea of creating a chancellor was first put forward by Emacia after winning the April 2023 general election. The position was first contested at the September 2023 general election with Emacia comfortably winning the seat.

Powers

The Constitution outlines the powers of the Chancellor. The Chancellor is the presiding authority over the presidium a3. When a matter requires a presidium vote and the vote is evenly split, whatever the Chancellor voted for prevails a6. The size of parliament before each election is decided by the Chancellor, so long as that number is between one tenth and one twentieth the size of the Discordian population a9.
