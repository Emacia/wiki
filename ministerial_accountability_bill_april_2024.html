Ministerial Accountability Bill, April 2024

Show Cause Provision

If a minister fails to fulfill their duties as outlined by their portfolio, a Member of Parliament (MP) or Premier may issue a show cause notice to the respective minister.

Reporting to Parliament

Upon issuance of a show cause notice, the respective minister must report to Parliament and explain the reason for failure to complete their duties.

Enforcement

Failure to comply with the show cause notice may result in further parliamentary inquiry and potential disciplinary actions as deemed appropriate by Parliament. After two warnings issued by Parliament, the minister will be removed from office.
