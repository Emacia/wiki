Transparency in Post-Election Data Disclosure Act, October 2023

  • The results of elections to be made public after said election with the removal of all names and other identification that may appear.

  • The order of the ballots are to be shuffled as to avoid identification from voting order.

  • The data is to be distributed by the Department of Data and Statistics
