Channels Backup Act, April 2024

All the channels will have a backup, to prevent their loss in cases of emergency.

The backup will happen in an independent reserve server, that will be used in cases of any attack against the main server.

The retrieve server shall have a list of all the members of Discordia.

All members in Discordia shall be invited to the reserve server.
