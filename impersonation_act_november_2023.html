Impersonation Act, November 2023

Section 1: Impersonation of Government Officials

(a) Any individual found guilty of impersonating a government official in the Republic of Discordia shall be subject to penalties as outlined in this Act.

(b) Impersonation of government officials includes, but is not limited to, falsely claiming to be an elected official, government employee, or any individual representing an official capacity within Discordia.

Section 2: Penalties

(a) Minimum Penalty: Any person found guilty of impersonating a government official shall be subject to a minimum punishment of a one-week timeout from participation in any official government channels, groups, or platforms.

(b) Maximum Penalty: The maximum punishment for impersonating a government official shall be a permanent ban from participating in official government channels, groups, or platforms.

(c) The penalty exercised shall be decided by a vote of the judiciary by a two thirds majority.

Section 3: Exceptions

(a) Satirical and Joking Exemption: This Act does not apply to individuals engaging in satirical or humorous impersonations of government officials as long as the intent is clearly humorous and not intended to deceive or cause harm.
