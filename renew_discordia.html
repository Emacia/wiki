History

Renew Discordia is currently the youngest party in the Republic of Discordia. It was founded on the 15th of January, 2024. Their goal is to establish a Parliamentary Democracy in Discordia, instead of the current Presidential system. They also claim to fight the percieved corruption and complacency in the Technosocialist Party
