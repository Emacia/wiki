The Attorney General Appointment and Responsibilities Act, November 2023

The Attorney General Appointment and Responsibilities Act establishes that the Attorney General of Discordia will be appointed by the Chancellor with the approval of the Presidium. The appointed Attorney General shall meet the criteria laid out in the Court competency Test (CCT) for the position. The Attorney General's primary duties include serving as the legal advisor to the government and representing the government's interests in court. It is stipulated that the Attorney General cannot hold any other positions concurrently.
