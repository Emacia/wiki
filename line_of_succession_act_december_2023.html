Line of Succession Act, December 2023

In the event that an authority figure is needed due to causal vacancy or not being online, the following line of succession shall be established. The highest person on the list who is active at any moment will be first point of contact within the server

  1. Chancellor
  2. Prime Minister
  3. Premier
  4. Chief Justice
  5. Deputy Prime Minister
  6. Deputy Premier
  7. Deputy Chief Justice
  8. Head Administrator
  9. Longest Serving Moderator
  10. Subsequent Moderators
  11. Leader of the House
  12. Longest Serving Member of Parliament
  13. Subsequent Members of Parliament by Time in Office or Time in Server if Tied.
  14. Minister of Moderation
  15. Minister of Data & Statistics
  16. Minister of Clubs
  17. Minister of Communications & Media
  18. Minister of Events
  19. Minister of Recruitment
  20. Minister of Elections
  21. Longest serving Court Justice
  22. Subsequent Court Justices by Time in Office or Time in Server if Tied.
