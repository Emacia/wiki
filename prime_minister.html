Introduction

The Prime Minister is the Head of the Legislative branch of Discordia. They are a presidium member and directly elected by the people at general elections.

History

Early in Discordia's history, the Prime Minister occupied a different role than it does today. The Prime Minister held infinite power and was the head of state as the Constitution hadn't been enacted yet. After the September 2023 general elections, the position became what it is today.

Duties and powers

The Prime Minister, along with the Chancellor has the power to determine the size of parliamenta9. During a Motion of no confidence the Prime Minister's vote is influential as without it, two thirds of parliament is needed for the vote. However a motion against the Prime Minister only requires half of parliamenta11.

In Parliament

The Prime Minister automatically given a seat in parliament despite being elected separately to other members of parliament. The Prime Minister is responsible for securing a majority in parliament to form government. The Prime Minister also selects the Deputy Prime Minister.
