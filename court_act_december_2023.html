Court Act, December 2023

1: In order for any citizen who is accused of breaking a law to be found guilty, there must be enough evidence to prove their guilt beyond any reasonable doubt.

2: Until found guilty, all accused citizens are assumed to be innocent.

3: All accused citizens are entitled to a lawyer/defence attorney for their trial if they so choose to have one. A lawyer must have passed an official test in order to act as lawyer.
