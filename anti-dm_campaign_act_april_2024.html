Anti-DM Campaign Act, April 2024

Prohibition
  • Direct Messaging (DM) individuals without prior consent to vote for a specific party or individual is strictly prohibited.
  • All election campaigns must occur in public assemblies.
    • Penalties

    The penalty applies solely to the individual initiating the unauthorized direct messaging campaign. The penalties shall be as follows;

    Minimum Sentence: Disqualification from the election.

    Maximum Sentence: Ban from participating in future elections.
