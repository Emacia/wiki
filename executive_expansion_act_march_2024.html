Executive Expansion Act, March 2024/h1>

The Department of Education shall be established to educate new members on how the server works and help facilitate their assimilation into Discordia, including its rules and culture. It shall also educate citizens on current projects within the server and educate them on new changes in tandem with the Department of Communications & Media.

The Department of Culture shall be established to develop, preserve and record Discordian history and culture through means such as the Wiki, Discordian Language, Handbook and other such projects.
